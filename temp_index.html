Our vision

Allied Priority Care is proudly an Australian owned and operated organisation. We're committed to creating a community where all individuals are recognised for their unique abilities and have their needs taken care of by an expert team. As a large Australian-owned enterprise, we’re passionate about caring for you in the most meaningful way and assisting you to achieve your dreams, no matter how big or small they might be. 

Experience is our middle name

One of the most outstanding attributes of our team is that we have a wealth of experience between us. Since we also operate registered training and labour hire organisations, we know how important expertise and qualifications are in delivering exceptional services.


We understand the needs of people living with disability and are able to provide the right kind of support so you can enjoy your life in the most fulfilling way.


Caring for someone with a unique ability is not the same as other forms of care and our team knows exactly what this means and what specific supports are required to help you overcome your daily challenges.


Our team is multi-lingual, and we specialise in trauma recovery for youth in residential care.

Our mission and values

At Allied Priority Care our mission is to provide individualised support to people living with a disability. We want you to experience a dignified life through our inclusive and collaborative services.


Our doors are always open and we welcome referrals from community workers or health professionals in Melbourne or Regional Victoria. Our values include:

Dignity & Respect

Being respectful of diversity and protecting the dignity of participants 

Quality

Support that is competent, timely, and individualised

Choice & Accountability

Enabling individuals to have control over the decisions that affect them. Remaining accountable to each participant for the work we do.
