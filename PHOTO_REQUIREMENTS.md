## PHOTO GUIDELINES

### General Requirements:
1. **Quality**: High resolution, professional photography preferred
2. **Diversity**: Ensure diverse representation (age, gender, cultural background, abilities)
3. **Authenticity**: Real photos preferred over stock photos (with proper permissions)
4. **Consistency**: Similar lighting and style across all photos
5. **Permissions**: Must have proper model releases and permissions for all photos
6. **Accessibility**: Photos should be inclusive and show people with disabilities positively

### Technical Specifications:
- **Format**: JPEG or PNG
- **Color Space**: RGB
- **Compression**: Optimized for web (balance quality and file size)
- **Aspect Ratios**: Maintain specified dimensions for best display

### Content Guidelines:
- Show genuine care and connection
- Professional but warm and approachable
- Inclusive and diverse
- Positive, empowering imagery
- Avoid stereotypes
- Show independence and support working together
